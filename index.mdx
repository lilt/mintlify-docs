## What is LILT?

LILT is an advanced localization platform that combines cutting-edge technology and human expertise to provide enterprise-grade translation services. Our platform revolutionizes the traditional translation workflow by integrating contextual AI with human translators to deliver faster, more accurate, and cost-effective localization solutions.

## Why Choose LILT?

Our platform moves beyond traditional Machine Translation Post-Editing (MTPE) by providing interactive and adaptive Contextual AI that empowers human translators. This unique approach results in:

- **Enhanced Accuracy**: AI learns in real-time from translator corrections
- **Increased Speed**: Optimized workflows reduce translation time
- **Cost Efficiency**: Better quality means fewer revision cycles
- **Scalability**: End-to-end localization solution that grows with your needs
