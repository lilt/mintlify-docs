## Translation Options

- **Text/Document**: Ready to use - click "Translate"
- **Image Files**: Translates text within images using OCR technology
- **Audio File**: Converts spoken words to text and translates audio files
- **Scanned PDF**: Extracts and translates text from scanned PDF documents
- **Video File**: Translates video content for global audiences
